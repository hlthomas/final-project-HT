This is Harlow

Singer/Songwriter | O'ahu -> NYC

Harlow is a 21-year-old indie/alternative singer-songwriter from O’ahu, Hawai’i. She is a self-taught musician who plays the guitar, piano, and ukulele. She draws inspiration from alternative and indie bands like The 1975 and The Front Bottoms. New music coming soon!
